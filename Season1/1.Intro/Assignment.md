When **Ryan Dahl** developed NodeJS, he used **Spider Monkey** (Firefox's JS engine) to build it.

**Joyent Inc.** funded NodeJS initially.
Initial name of NodeJS was **web.js**.

In **2012**, after 3 years into development, **Ryan** stepped back and **Isaac** (developer of NPM) started to manage Node.
The development slowed down.

In **2014**, an individual developer **Fedor** forked Node as **io.js**, worked on it, and it was eventually **merged in 2015**.

From here onwards, it was **not managed by Joyent Inc.** but by the **NodeJS Foundation Committee**.

In **2019**, the **OpenJS Foundation Committee**
(JS Foundation Committee + NodeJS Foundation Committee) took control over NodeJS since then.
